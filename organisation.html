Organisational Structure

Board of Directors

The Board of Directors oversees the overall strategic direction and policies of the organization.

Executive Management Team

The Executive Management Team consists of key executives responsible for implementing the decisions of the Board and managing day-to-day operations.

Departments

The organization is structured into various departments, each responsible for specific functions:

  • Finance Department: Manages financial resources, budgeting, and accounting.
  • Human Resources Department: Handles recruitment, employee relations, and training.
  • Marketing Department: Develops marketing strategies and promotional activities.
  • Operations Department: Oversees production, logistics, and supply chain management.
  • IT Department: Manages information technology infrastructure and systems.
  • Research and Development Department: Conducts research and innovation initiatives.

Regional Offices

Regional offices are established to oversee operations and coordination within specific geographic regions.

Support Units

In addition to departments, there are support units such as:

  • Administrative Support: Provides administrative and logistical support to all departments.
  • Customer Service: Handles customer inquiries and support services.
  • Legal and Compliance: Ensures adherence to legal regulations and compliance standards.

Reporting Structure

Reporting lines are established from each department to the Executive Management Team, ensuring accountability and communication.
